Mock Interview Series

The Mock Interview Series prepares and familiarizes students with the Technical Interview Experience and Environment. These mock interviews are crucial and impactful for students who are nearing the interview and placement season or have any upcoming interviews in product-based companies. It also builds confidence in students, lose the fear of interviews and help them to ace the next coding interview. Get yourselves interviewed from a panel of our students currently employed in top product-based companies. Learn from the experience of our students and follow the foot-steps of these students to fulfill your dream of getting placed in product based company.

Process of Conducting Mock Interviews

1. Online Coding Test:

Get the experience of initial shortlisting rounds of technical interview by testing your coding skills. Go through the same format as chosen by the top product-based companies for shortlisting candidates.This round comprises of 10-20 MCQ’s on theoretical concepts such as Operating Systems, DBMS, System Design, OOPs and 2-3 coding questions based on Data Structure and Algorithms.

2. Technical Interview Based on Data Structures:

Get the real vibe of a technical face-to-face interview with the second round of the mock series. With this round, get the opportunity to simulate a real-world coding round with employees from top product-based companies such as Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs and many other.Gaining such an experience of face to face interviews, students learn the do’s and don’ts of a technical interview and make them less prone to mistakes which might have severe impact on the interview result.

3. Technical Interview on Core Computers:

Test your knowledge of theoretical computer science subjects such as Operating Systems, DBMS, System Design, Object Oriented Programming and more such topics. The theoretical subjects are neglected by many students for such technical interviews but prove to be a deciding factor in the interview result.Gain an edge over others by analyzing and noting the areas of improvement in these subjects and maximize the chances of clearing the core computers round of every interview.

4. Feedback and Counselling:

The final round of the series to sum up the entire interview process and give valuable feedback to the students according to the performance based on the previous three rounds. With this feedback, the students can work on improving certain areas according to interview standards and improve the overall interview experience. The students recognize the weak areas of preparation and get the overall vibe and experience of the interview process.
